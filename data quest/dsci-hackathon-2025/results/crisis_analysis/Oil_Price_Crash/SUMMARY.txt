Oil Price Crash (2014-2016)
======================================================================

CAUSE OF CRISIS:
Oil prices collapsed from $110 to $30 per barrel due to oversupply and weak demand. Alberta entered recession with thousands of layoffs in oil & gas, causing ripple effects across Western Canada including Vancouver's real estate and service sectors.
